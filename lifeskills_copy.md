## SECTION 6 — FAQ
**Title:** Frequently asked questions.

**Q: What is LifeSkills?**
LifeSkills is a live coaching platform where you learn mind-body practices like breathwork, meditation, and yoga with an experienced teacher, one-on-one. We teach you the skill of self-regulation so you can create lasting peace of mind on your own.

**Q: How is this different from therapy or meditation apps?**
Therapy often focuses on talking through problems. Apps give you pre-recorded content. LifeSkills gives you a real teacher who works with you live to build practical skills you carry with you for life. It's not about coping — it's about learning to operate your own mind and body.

**Q: How does a live session work?**
You meet your teacher over video for a live, one-on-one session. They'll talk with you about where you are, then guide you through a practice tailored to your needs. You leave every session with something you can use on your own.

**Q: Who are the professionals? Are they certified?**
Our teachers are certified practitioners in breathwork, meditation, yoga, and other mind-body disciplines. Every teacher is vetted for experience, credentials, and their ability to connect and teach effectively.

**Q: Do I need any prior experience?**
None. Our teachers work with complete beginners and experienced practitioners alike. Every session meets you exactly where you are.

**Q: Can I try it before committing?**
Yes. Book a free intro call with any teacher to see if it's the right fit. No pressure, no commitment.
