Brand Voice

Rules for all copy on the platform. Applied to every section, label, CTA, and error message.

RuleDoDon't
Tone Calm, direct, warm Clinical, salesy, hype-y
Sentence length Short. One idea per sentence. Long run-on descriptions
Focus Transformation ("Walk away with real skills") Feature lists ("Our platform offers...")
Section structure One H2 title per section only Subtitles, taglines under titles
Terminology Teachers, coaches, sessions, skills Practitioners, therapists, treatment
